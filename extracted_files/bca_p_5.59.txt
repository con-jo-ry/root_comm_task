punas tad ekāntam avadhārayituṃ kāyapratyavekṣām āha.

kāyasya sarvathā kvacid api vyāpāro nāsti, svātmany api sāmarthyābhāvāt. anyathā cittarahito mṛtasya kāyaḥ. gṛdhrādibhir vipralujyamāna itastataḥ pratikāram ātmarakṣaṇārthaṃ kim iti na karotīti pṛcchati sarvasāmarthyavikalatvāt. ata eva cittaparikarmaiva sādhyam. tasmin parikarmite kāyasyāyatnata eva parikarmasiddheḥ, tatparatantratvāt tasyety uktaṃ bhavati.
