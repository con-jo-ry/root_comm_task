kṛtvetyādi. ṣaṇṇāṃ pakṣāṇām anyatamasya phalasya sādhyatvād yad yad evābhiṣṭaṃ tad eva sākṣāt kṛtvā, paścāt sarvathaiva pradīpavan nirodha uttarakālaṃ sattvārthādiśūnyaḥ sākṣāt kartavyaḥ.

nanu ṣaṭpakṣabhedena ṣaḍ eva nirodhāḥ syuḥ. tat katham eka eva nirodha ity āśaṅkyāha—abhinnetyādi. abhinnaṃ rūpaṃ yasya sa tathā. na hi nirodhānāṃ ṣaṭpakṣalakṣaṇabhede 'pi bhedo 'sti, abhāvaikarūpatayā nirodhasya samānatvāt. ayam arthaḥ—anyatamapakṣaṃ sākṣāt kṛtvā, paścāt tasya santānocchedarūpo nirodha iti saptamaṃ sādhyam.
