tad eva dauṣprajñavikṣepāsamyakpraṇidhivipakṣābhibhūtirahitatvenāha mahādhyānetyādi |

tatrāsyāṃ gāthāyāṃ dhyānaprajñayor vyatyayo draṣṭavyaḥ | tataś cāyam arthaḥ | mahāprajñāśarīradhṛg iti vajradhūpā | mahati prajñā mahāprajñā saiva śarīraṃ tad dhārayatīti mahāprajñāśarīradhṛk || mahādhyānasamādhistha iti vajrapuṣpā | samādhiś cittasyaikāgratālakṣaṇas tasmiṃs tiṣṭhatīti samādhisthaḥ | mahac ca tad dhyānaṃ ca mahādhyānaṃ mahattvaṃ caturdhyānātmakatvāt | teṣu caturṣu dhyāneṣu samādhisthaḥ || praṇidhir iti vajradīpā | praṇidhīyata iti praṇidhiḥ | bodhisattvānāṃ praṇidhānavaśād utpatteḥ || mahopāya iti vajragandhā | mahāṃś cāsāv upāyaś ca mahopāyaḥ | tata evopāyabalenāśeṣānavaśeṣasya sattvadhātor yathāśayābhiprāyaparipūraṇāt || 

ata eva prajñādhyānapraṇidhyupāyapāramitālakṣaṇatayā bāhyapūjācatuṣṭayātmakam iti ||

sāmbhogikamārgaprītihetubhāvena balajñānayor dharmadhātvavatārasvabhāvatvāt tayor eva balajñānayoḥ pravibhāgaḥ | tatra balaṃ śraddhādipañca | jñānaṃ darśanamārge bhāvanāmārge ca | tatra darśanamārge bodhyaṅgeṣu | bhāvanāmārga āryāṣtāṅgeṣu || 

tataś ca śraddhāvīryasmṛtisamādhirūpatayā dvārapālacatuṣṭayātmakaṃ mahāyānābhisampratyayapravṛttitannairantaryasantuṣṭikāraṇatvāt ||
