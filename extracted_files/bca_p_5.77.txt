paraguṇāmarṣaṇaṃ vārayann āha-

sarveṣāṃ hīnamadhyotkṛṣṭānāṃ sattvānām, sarve vopakramāḥ duḥkhaparihāreṇa tuṣṭayarthāḥ. sarvārambhapariśrameṇa tuṣṭir evotpādayitavyety arthaḥ. sā ca tuṣṭir dhanavisargair api durlabhā, syād vā na veti. iha punar ayatnasiddhopasthitā kasmāt parihīyata iti matvā bhokṣya anubhaviṣyāmi saṃtoṣasukham anyayatnaniṣpāditair guṇair upanāmitam. na hi priyaputraguṇair akṣamāyuktā. iti bhāvanayā paraguṇaśravaṇāc cittakārkaśyam apakuryāt.
