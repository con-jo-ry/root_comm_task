evaṃ tāvad anayor anvayavyatirekābhyāṃ guṇadoṣāv abhidhāya, anarthavivarjanārthaṃ niṣphalaspandavarjanam āha.

prathamaṃ tāvad idaṃ cittam ity adhyātmani cintayati. sarvakālam īdṛśam uktakramayuktam upasthāpayitavyam. tataḥ paraṃ niṣphalaspandavarjanārtham apagatakaraṇagrāmeṇeva niṣphalarūpādiviṣayagrahaṇasarvavikalpopasaṃhārān mayā sthātavyam. kim iva? kāṣṭhavat, cakṣurādivyāpāraśūnyatvāt.
