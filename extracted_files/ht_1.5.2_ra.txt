tatpunarasukaramupāyatattvamantareṇa sākṣātkartumiti yathā tadupāyopetaṃ vajrayānāśrayeṇa sākṣātkartavyaṃ taddarśayitumāha—jananīmityādi |

tatra jananī abhiṣekadāyikā | bhaginī vajrayānasamārūḍhā | tatsthānīyā cetyeke | nītārthaṃ tu svayamākhyāsyati | yogaḥ śūnyatākaruṇayoḥ | taṃ vettīti yogavit | yogavitsanpūjayet | yathā yogaṃ jānīyāttathā pūjayediti |

kāsau tathābhūtetyāha—naṭītyādi | naṭanarañjanagāyanacaṇḍatābrahmapriyatāguṇayuktāstathoktā ityeke | prajñā śūnyatārūpā yoginyaḥ | upāyaḥ śrīherukaḥ | tayorvidhānena mudrāyāmātmani cādhimokṣāt | pūjayedi toṣayet |

	haṭhena yoginīyogādyogī sthūlātyayo ||

yataścāha—

	tāvatsevyate prajñā yāvacchukravatī bhavet |

iti | tattvamiha prajñopāyamayam | statpriyaḥ sanna tu rāgitādibhiḥ |
