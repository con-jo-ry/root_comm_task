sa hi śayyāṃ parikalpayannevaṃ parikalpayedityupadarśayannāha-

bhagavato mahānirvāṇaśayyām iva śayyāṃ parikalpayet. abhimatayā diśā śiro vidhāya, dakṣiṇena pārśvena, pādasyopari pādam ādhāya, dakṣiṇaṃ bāhum upadhānaṃ kṛtvā, vāmaṃ ca prasārya jaṅghopari niveśya, cīvaraiḥ susaṃvṛtakāyaḥ, smṛtaḥ, saṃprajānānaḥ, utthānasaṃjñī, ālokasaṃjñī, śayitaḥ, nācittakamiddhāvaṣṭabdhaḥ. na ca nidrāsukham ākhādayet, na ca pārśvasukham anyatra yāvad evaiṣāṃ mahābhūtānāṃ sthitaye yāpanāyai iti. laghutthānaḥ śīghram evottiṣṭhet. na tu jṛmbhikāṃ gātramoṭanaṃ kurvann ālasyopahitaś cireṇa. etebhya eva sarvebhyaḥ pūrvam eva.
