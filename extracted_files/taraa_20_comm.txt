gambhīraśūnyaṃ pratibhāsamātraṃ śāntātisūkṣmam anabhilāpyaṃ nirlepaṃ nīrupam nirañjanādi, ādiśabdāt śivaṃ nirākāraṃ niṣprapañcam anādinidhanam ityādiśabdair bhrāntir na kartavyāparasādhyasattve 'parasya sādhyasya sattve sattāyām. ebhiḥ śabdair eva param api kiñcit sādhyaṃ kathitād astīti bhrāntir na kartavyā. athavā nātikathitam eva sādhyam ebhiḥ sarvair abhidhīyata iti niścayaḥ.
