śikśāsamuccaye 'pi bodhisattvānāṃ karaṇīyam upadiṣṭam iti tad api nirūpaṇīyam ity āha.

śikśāsamuccayo 'pi svayam ebhir eva kṛtaḥ. avaśyaṃ niyamena. draṣṭavyaḥ punaḥpunar asakṛt, abhyasanīya iti bhāvaḥ. kutaḥ? yasmāt satāṃ bodhisattvānām ācaraṇam ācāra itikartavyatā. tatra śikṣāsamuccaye vistareṇa prabandhena pradarśitaḥ vispaṣṭīkṛtya prakāśitaḥ.
