kṛtvetyādi. svādhipaṃ sveṣṭadaivataṃ sākṣāt kṛtvāmukhīkṛtya sātarūpaṃ sukhaikasvabhāvam, paścād devatākāraṃ parityajya, sukhamātraṃ phalaṃ sādhyaṃ vyavasthitaṃ syāt.

nanu yadi sākṣāt kṛtvāpi devatākāras tyaktavyaḥ, tarhi prathamam eva kasmād vibhāvitaḥ? sukhamātram eva dvitīyasādhyavat kiṃ na vibhāvitam? kiṃ vṛthāprayāsenety āha—śuddham ityādi. śuddhaṃ kevalaṃ devatākāravirahitaṃ sukhamātraṃ naiva sākṣāt kartuṃ śakyate, ākārarahitasya sukhasyānupalambhāt. tasmāt tena kāraṇenākāro bhāvitaḥ svādhipasyeti tṛtīyam. ayam arthaḥ—devatākārasaṃvalitam eva sukhaṃ vibhāvya, sākṣātkṛte devatākāraṃ tyaktvā, sukhamātram eva sādhyam uktaguṇam.
